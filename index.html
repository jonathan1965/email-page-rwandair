JAN-MARCH 2022 ISSUE

Letter from the editor,

RwandAir is delighted to share with you the first issue of its new quarterly newsletter. We will bring you the latest news, partnerships and exciting stories straight from one of the world’s fastest growing airlines.

RwandAir currently operates a young and modern fleet of 12 aircraft with an average age of just under six years. We offer services to 28 destinations throughout Africa, Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

Earlier this year, RwandAir became the first African airline to be awarded the top Diamond status rating for COVID-19 prevention measures - the highest attainable level.

The award comes from APEX Health Safety, powered by SimpliFlying, for our sustained work to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness and sanitation for you, our loyal customers and dedicated crew.

The Diamond rating recognises RwandAir as the safest airline in Africa and comes after RwandAir recently announced it was the first African airline to vaccinate all staff.

APEX Health Safety, powered by SimpliFlying, sets the industry benchmark for customer-centric COVID-19 certification.

New routes

You may have seen us recently start two new routes from Kigali to the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) and now Doha in Qatar. We are also operating a new twice-weekly service to Lubumbashi, the DRC’s mining capital, and another to Goma - capital of North Kivu province. Our new Doha route operates three weekly flights you can take advantage of our three month offer of fares from £550. The new route comes just in time for the FIFA 2022 World Cup in Qatar in November, providing the perfect opportunity to enjoy one of the new direct flights. The new routes can be booked directly through rwandair.com. Charter flights for the new route can be booked via travel agents or directly by emailing charters@rwandair.com. We’re always looking to expand our route network and offer our customers even more choice and flexibility when it comes to flying with RwandAir and 2022 will be no exception, we have a European hotspot in the pipeline which we’ll be revealing soon

New Partnerships

Qatar

We’re really excited to share with you details of our new partnership with Qatar Airways, as the first Sub-Saharan African airline to enter into partnership with Qatar Airways’ Privilege Club, RwandAir is incredibly proud to launch this joint loyalty programme. The new partnership means that if you’re a RwandAir Dream Miles customer, you are able to earn and burn points giving you access to flights across Qatar Airways’ extensive route network of more than 200 destinations, with the same reciprocal benefits applying to Qatar’s Privilege Club customers. We’ve also entered into an interline agreement with Qatar Airways, which means RwandAir customers will be able to easily explore even more of the world, as customers will have access to the networks of both airlines providing seamless travel experiences. Customers can now pick and choose from over 160 destinations in the combined networks of both airlines, which are perfectly connected via the main hubs in Kigali and Doha. You can sign up https://bit.ly/3wvWP6h to join the RwandAir Dream Miles loyalty programme today and enjoy our double miles offering on the new Doha route, with the promotional deal ending March 2022. We also have a new promotion: emerald or silver dream miles members can now access the doha lounge with just 2500 miles promo runs till march 2022. For more information dreamiles@rwandair.com

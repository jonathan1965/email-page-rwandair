New routes

You may have seen us recently start two new routes from Kigali to the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) and now Doha in Qatar. The new route comes just in time for the FIFA 2022 World Cup in Qatar in November, providing the perfect opportunity to enjoy one of the new direct flights. The new routes can be booked directly through rwandair.com. Charter flights for the new route can be booked via travel agents or directly by emailing charters@rwandair.com.
